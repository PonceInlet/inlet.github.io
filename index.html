About

Band Story

INLET formed in Port Orange, Florida when five high-school friends turned surf days and skate parks into a band. They write sun-soaked, hook-driven songs about good times, love, and life — blending shimmering reverb and chorus-soaked guitars with tight rhythms, vocal interplay, and contemporary indie textures.

With an unmistakable three-guitar attack, INLET balances nostalgic surf warmth and modern alt-rock energy, delivering sets that feel equally at home in packed clubs, intimate theaters, DIY rooms, and coastal festivals.

Technical

Live Setup & Tech Rider

Show Format

Typical set: 30–75 minutes. 5-piece lineup: 3 electric guitars, bass, drums.
Flexible for support, co-headline, or festival slots.

Preferred Backline

  • 4-piece drum kit + hi-hat, ride, crash
  • 3× small/medium guitar amps (30–100W), Fender-style preferred
  • 1× bass rig (250W+ head/combo) or DI to house

Suggested Input List

  • 1 — Kick
  • 2 — Snare
  • 3 — Hi-Hat
  • 4 — Overhead L
  • 5 — Overhead R
  • 6 — Bass DI
  • 7 — Guitar 1 (Vox) L
  • 8 — Guitar 2 (Vox) R
  • 9 — Guitar 3
  • 10 — Backing Vocal 1
  • 11 — Backing Vocal 2
  • 12 — FX / Spare
